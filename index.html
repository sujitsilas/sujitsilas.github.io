About Me

I'm a second-year MS student in Bioengineering at UCLA, with a focus on biomedical data sciences. My research interests span single-cell genomics, deep learning applications in biology, and cancer genomics. I’m currently working with data related to differential methylation and methylation patterns in cancer at GRAIL.
